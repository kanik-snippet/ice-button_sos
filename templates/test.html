Transforming Healthcare Security with ICE-Button

An Advanced Emergency Response System for Healthcare Facilities

Executive Summary

The healthcare sector has become increasingly vulnerable to violent incidents, jeopardizing the safety of staff, patients, and visitors. Recognizing the urgent need for a comprehensive security system, Mobiloitte Technologies Pvt Ltd developed ICE-Button, an advanced Emergency Response System specifically designed for healthcare facilities.

ICE-Button ensures swift, multi-channel communication and real-time situational awareness during emergencies. With its intuitive interface, the system reduces incident escalation and improves response times, safeguarding lives and property while restoring confidence in healthcare environments.

The ICE-Button Solution

ICE-Button is an end-to-end emergency response systemcombining advanced technology with real-time communication capabilities to mitigate safety risks. Designed specifically for healthcare settings, ICE-Button ensures that healthcare professionals can quickly activate alerts, escalate incidents, and monitor events in real time.


Key Features


  1. Instant Activation:
    • A single press of the red SOS button triggers immediate emergency alerts.
  2. Multi-Channel Notifications:
    • Alerts are sent simultaneously via SMS, WhatsApp, email, and voice calls to predefined contacts.
  3. Real-Time Monitoring:
    • Live video and audio streaming provides continuous situational updates during an incident.
  4. Post-Incident Reporting:
    • Automatically generates detailed reports with recorded evidence for documentation and analysis.
  5. Ease of Use:
    • An intuitive user interface designed to ensure ease of operation for healthcare professionals.

How ICE-Button Works

Press the Button

Press the ICE SOS Button during an emergency to send an immediate alert to pre-configured contacts.

Instant Notification

Your contacts receive an alert with your location, ensuring quick assistance is on its way.

Continuous Monitoring

The device continues to track and notify contacts until the situation is resolved.



  1. Step 1: Trigger Alerts
    • Press the ICE SOS button to initiate emergency notifications.
  2. Step 2: Send Notifications:
    • Notifications are sent to up to 15 pre-configured contacts via multiple communication channels (SMS, email, WhatsApp, and voice calls).
  3. Step 3: Continuous Monitoring:
    • Real-time video and audio feeds are streamed to provide immediate situational awareness.
  4. Step 4: Acknowledge Alerts:
    • Responders confirm receipt of the alert and initiate necessary actions.
  5. Step 5: Resolve:
    • Situations are marked as resolved using the blue button upon completion of emergency handling.
  6. Step 6: Incident Reporting:
    • A comprehensive post-incident report with recorded audio, video, and alert logs is generated for documentation.
